To issue the identity for a participant:- 

command :-  composer identity issue -c admin@tutorial-network -f TRADER4.card -u TRADER4 -a "resource:org.example.mynetwork.Trader#TRADER4"
Here TRADER4 is the id of paticipant.

To List the identity registry
command :- composer identity list -c admin@tutorial-network  //( admin@tutorial-network is cardname)
